Boost Your Speed and Agility

Speed is crucial for athletes in almost every sport. Here are the techniques and exercises that will help you become faster and more explosive.

Speed Training Plan:

  • Short sprints (10-30 meters)
  • Agility drills (ladder drills, cone drills)
  • Hill sprints for power development
  • Speed endurance (repeat sprints with rest)
  • Plyometric exercises (box jumps, bounding)
